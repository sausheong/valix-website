About Valix

Valix represents a breakthrough in personal health monitoring, bringing medical-grade technology to your everyday life. Our app uses sophisticated computer vision algorithms to analyze subtle changes in skin color caused by blood flow, allowing us to estimate vital signs without any physical contact.

Built on years of research in remote photoplethysmography, Valix makes health monitoring accessible, convenient, and completely private. Whether you're tracking your wellness journey or just curious about your body's signals, Valix provides insights you can trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How does Valix measure heart rate without touching?

Valix uses a technology called remote photoplethysmography (rPPG). Your phone's camera detects subtle changes in skin color caused by blood flow changes. Our algorithms analyze these color variations to estimate your heart rate and blood pressure.

What do I need for accurate measurements?

For best results:

  • Good, even lighting (avoid harsh shadows or direct sunlight)
  • Stay still during measurement
  • Ensure your face is clearly visible in the camera
  • Remove glasses if they cause glare
  • Clean camera lens for clear images

How accurate are the measurements?

Valix provides estimates for wellness purposes only. Accuracy varies based on lighting conditions, movement, and individual characteristics. While many users find results helpful for tracking trends, Valix is not a medical device and should not be used for medical diagnosis.

Technical Issues

Why am I getting "No Reliable Reading"?

This usually happens when:

  • Too much movement during measurement
  • Poor lighting conditions (too dark or too bright)
  • Face tracking issues (face not clearly visible)
  • Camera lens is dirty or smudged

Try improving lighting conditions, staying still, and ensuring your face is clearly visible.

The app isn't detecting my face properly

Try these solutions:

  • Ensure good, even lighting on your face
  • Remove face masks or obstructions
  • Position your face in the center of the frame
  • Hold the phone steady at eye level
  • Clean the camera lens
  • Restart the app if issues persist

How do I sync with Apple Health?

To enable Apple Health sync:

  1. Go to Settings in the app
  2. Toggle "Apple Health" to on
  3. Grant permission when prompted
  4. If already enabled, try turning it off and on again

Note: You'll need to grant permission for both heart rate and blood pressure data.

Privacy & Data

Where is my health data stored?

All your health data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers or third parties. Only you have access to your measurements. If you enable Apple Health sync, data is shared only with Apple Health on your device.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export all your measurement history from the Settings screen. Tap "Export All Data" to receive a CSV file with your complete measurement history that you can save or share.

Is my camera data recorded or stored?

No. The camera feed is processed in real-time and is not recorded or stored. Only the numerical measurement results are saved to your device.

Troubleshooting Guide

Poor Results

  • Improve lighting - use indirect, even light
  • Reduce movement - stay completely still
  • Clean camera lens
  • Remove glasses if causing glare
  • Ensure face fills the detection area

App Crashes

  • Force quit and restart the app
  • Check for iOS updates
  • Restart your device
  • Free up device storage space
  • Contact support if issues persist

Battery Drain

  • Close app when not measuring
  • Enable low power mode during extended use
  • Reduce screen brightness
  • Keep iOS updated for optimal performance

Health Sync Issues

  • Check Apple Health permissions
  • Toggle Health integration off/on
  • Ensure iOS Health app is updated
  • Restart both apps
  • Check iOS privacy settings

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Data Collection

Valix is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal health data. All measurements are processed and stored locally on your device.

Camera Usage

The camera is used only during active measurements. Camera feed is processed in real-time and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted. No images or videos are saved by the app.

Local Storage

Your measurement history is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. You have full control over this data and can export or delete it at any time.

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, measurement data is shared only with the Apple Health app on your device. This integration is optional and requires your explicit permission.

Data Security

All data is encrypted using iOS standard encryption methods. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, and delete all your data at any time through the app settings.
